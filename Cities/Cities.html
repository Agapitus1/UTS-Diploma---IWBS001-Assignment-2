LAS VEGAS

         As we can know, Las Vegas is one of many cities located in Nevada, United State of America. This city is known as a popular night city in USA. Las Vegas actually have nothing to look and have nothing special when you come in there at the noon. However, when you come to this city at night, you will know why people love to come to this cities. This is also the reason why I love Las Vegas. The city view at night going very crazy!   • Lounge by the pool and order bottle service
  • Have a drink and support your local bars
  • Explore the Fremont Street Experience
  • Check out the latest Secret Burger event

  • Try a new kind of Vegas buffet
  • Head out of town for a day trip
  • Soak in the beauty of the Bellagio fountains
  • Watch old signs come back to life at the Neon Museum
  • Explore the local neighborhood dining scene

And many more things you can do in Las Vegas! Just try to visit Las Vegas and you will know how great is Las Vegas!
